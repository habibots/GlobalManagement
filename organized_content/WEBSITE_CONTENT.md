### Main Content - Our Firm

The Company was established in 2004 by two accomplished engineers who amassed a vast wealth of experience internationally as well as locally. The founding Principals have participated in projects ranging from simple remodeling of space to complex airports and infrastructure projects. The strength of the company, technically, financially and ethically have allowed us to complete numerous project successfully and gain our clients trust, confidence and friendship. The wealth of experience of our team allows us to find creative solutions for construction challenges that might rise during the course of the project

### Company Overview

Global Management & Construction Corp. is a licensed General Contractor in the State of Nevada (Lic. 66668 with $6.8 million limit) Since 2006 and provides services in:

- Project development
- Project Management
- Design and build projects
- Commercial Construction
- New Developments
- Tenant improvement

### Service Details

**Project Development**

Our Principals are well experienced in the project development stages and aware of the details necessary for completing projects on time, within budget while maintaining standards of quality work.

**Initial Stage Involvement**

To Achieve the best outcome for our clients we avail our expertise to Architects, Engineers and designers to assist in the development of the project, based on the client's needs and requirements, providing cost saving and quality enhancement solutions at the initial project stages. The cumulative experience of our team has proved valuable to many of our clients.

**Permitting and Approval**

Our team is well conversed in building codes and practices and work diligently with different entities to efficiently obtain permits and approvals in timely manner conducive to smooth execution of the works.

**Construction Management**

We work with all entities to coordinate the process of construction in compliance with building codes and quality standards. Prepare a comprehensive schedules for construction and furnish progress reports to keep our clients abreast of the development of their project.

**Project Completion**

Projects coordination does not end with construction but with the clients receiving and operating their new premises. This entails assisting in obtaining all necessary approvals and certificates for their successful operation

## ABOUT PAGE (about.html)

### Our Philosophy

### Our Mission

Construction projects are orchestrated in meticulous planning, coordination and communications with all parties involved to ultimately achieve clients needs. To accomplish this goal we emphasize the need for:

- Involvement in the initial stages of the project
- Create a consultative model between owners, architects, engineers and other professionals to enhance communication
- Cost effectiveness and budgetary controls
- Effective and participative hands on job site management
- Attention to owners needs

Global Management & Construction Corp understands that the process is not simply construction and we made it our mission to strive to provide highest quality service to our clients in developing their projects efficiently, on time and to the highest quality. Our focus on core values of trustworthiness, honesty, dedication and reliability provides our client with unwavering commitment to their project as we pride ourselves in the satisfaction of our clients.
